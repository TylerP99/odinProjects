Carnitas

Description

Flavorful, delicious, and sure not to disapoint. Plus, these carnitas are cooked in an instant pot, so they come out much faster than other methods!

Ingredients

  • 1 (4-5 lb) boneless pork shoulder, quartered with excess fat trimmed off
  • 1 cup (dark) beer
  • 6 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup fresh orange juice
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 chipotle pepper
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • olive oil
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 white onion, quartered
  • For serving: cilantro, red onion, chipotle mayo, cheese

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, mix beer, garlic, orange juice, lime juice, cumin, oregano, salt, pepper, and chipotle pepper until combined, set aside.
  2. Season pork chunks on all sides with salt, pepper, and chili powder.
  3. Turn the instant pot on to "Saute" mode. Add oil and half the pork. Sear on each side for about a minute or until golden brown. Remove and set aside, repeat with other half of pork. Finally, with the instant pot empty, add onion and saute for about 3 minutes, adding oil if needed. Add in sauce prepared earlier and scrape the bottom of the pot to clean it off. Press "Cancel" to turn off the heat.
  4. Add pork back into the pot, trying to get each piece as submerged in the sauce as possible. Close lid and ensure vent is sealed.
  5. Cook on high pressure for 90 minutes then release the pressure.
  6. Open the instant pot, then use two forks to shred the pork. At this point, the chipotle pepper can be removed if found and discarded.
  7. Add a touch of olive oil to a frying pan and let it preheat some on medium-high.
  8. Cook pork in batches, adding in some of the remaining liquid whenever the pork dries out.
  9. Assemble tacos and serve!

Teriyaki Chicken

Description

Authentic, and delicious, teriyaki chicken. Simple and easy!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1/4 cup sake
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup mirin
  • 1/8 cup to 1/4 cup sugar, depending on how sweet you like it

Instructions

  1. Preheat a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Mix remaining ingredients together in a bowl, set aside for later
  3. Add chicken, cook for about 4 minutes, flip, cook for about 4 minutes.
  4. Add in teriyaki sauce from earlier, turn down heat to low, and let simmer until sauce thickens.
  5. Serve over rice!
